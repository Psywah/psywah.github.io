Shihua Gong

Research Associate

University of Bath

Biography

My research interests include scientific computing, mixed finite element methods, domain decomposition methods, multigrid methods and the nonlinearly preconditioned Newton methods. Now I am working with Ivan Graham and Euan Spence on fast solvers for frequency-domain wave-scattering problems.

Interests

  • scientific computing and computer simulation
  • linearly and nonlinearly preconditioning techniques
  • finite element methods and scalable solvers
